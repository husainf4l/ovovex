Absolutely. You maintain full control with the ability to review, correct, and approve all AI-suggested categorizations. The AI learns from your corrections to improve accuracy.

The AI can process receipts, invoices, bank statements, credit card statements, utility bills, and most business documents with printed or handwritten text.

How does the AI handle complex accounting scenarios?

The AI is trained on complex accounting rules and can handle multi-currency transactions, deferred revenue, inventory accounting, and industry-specific requirements.

Is my financial data secure with AI processing?

Yes, all data is encrypted and processed in secure environments. AI models are trained on anonymized data, and your sensitive financial information never leaves our secure infrastructure.
