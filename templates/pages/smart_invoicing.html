Smart Invoicing FAQ

Answers to common questions about our invoicing platform.

How quickly can I start sending invoices?

You can create and send your first invoice within minutes of signing up. Our templates are pre-configured, and you can import client data or add it manually to get started immediately.

What payment methods do you support?

We support credit cards, debit cards, ACH bank transfers, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and traditional payment methods. All payments are processed securely through PCI-compliant gateways.

Can I customize invoice templates?

Yes, you can fully customize invoice templates with your logo, colors, fonts, and layout. Professional plans include advanced branding options, while Enterprise plans offer complete template customization.

How do automated reminders work?

You can set up customizable reminder sequences that automatically send friendly reminders, payment updates, and escalation notices based on due dates and payment status. This typically improves collection rates by 30-50%.

Is my payment data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption, comply with PCI DSS standards, and never store sensitive payment information on our servers. All payment processing is handled through certified payment gateways.
