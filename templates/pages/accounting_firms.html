Accounting Firm FAQ

Answers to common questions from accounting firms.

How does client data security work?

Each client account is completely segregated with bank-grade encryption. Your firm maintains full control over client data access, and we comply with all major privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

Can I migrate my existing clients?

Yes! We provide comprehensive data migration tools and services. Our team can help import client data from QuickBooks, Xero, Excel files, and most accounting software platforms.

What level of customization is available?

Professional and Enterprise plans include white-label branding, custom workflows, and API access. Enterprise plans also include custom development services for unique requirements.

How does the billing work for multiple clients?

You pay one monthly fee for your firm account. Client billing is handled separately through our integrated invoicing system, with automated recurring charges and detailed time tracking.

Can I integrate with my existing software?

Yes, we offer integrations with major accounting software, CRM systems, and practice management tools. Our API-first architecture also supports custom integrations for specialized needs.
