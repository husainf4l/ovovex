How long does it take to set up Ovovex?

Most small businesses can get started in under 30 minutes. We provide guided setup wizards and import tools for your existing data.

Can I import my existing financial data?

Yes! We support importing from QuickBooks, Xero, Excel spreadsheets, and most accounting software. Our team can help with complex migrations.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and comply with SOC 2 Type II standards. Your data is safer with us than in most banks.

What kind of support do you provide?

All plans include email support with 24-hour response times. Professional and Premium plans include phone support and priority assistance.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation for export purposes.
