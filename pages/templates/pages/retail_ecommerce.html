Retail & E-commerce FAQ

Answers to common questions from retail and online business owners.

How does inventory tracking work across multiple locations?

Our platform provides real-time inventory synchronization across all your locations and sales channels. When an item sells in your online store, your physical store inventory updates automatically, preventing overselling and stockouts.

Can I integrate with my existing e-commerce platform?

Yes! We integrate with all major e-commerce platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, and Squarespace. We also support marketplace integrations with Amazon, eBay, and Walmart.

How do you handle sales tax for multiple states or countries?

Our platform automatically calculates sales tax based on customer location, product type, and applicable tax rates. We stay updated with the latest tax regulations and support both origin-based and destination-based tax calculations.

What kind of reporting do you provide for retail businesses?

We provide comprehensive retail-specific reports including sales by channel, inventory turnover, gross margin analysis, customer lifetime value, seasonal trends, and promotional campaign performance tracking.

Can I track customer behavior and purchase patterns?

Absolutely! Our customer analytics track purchase history, browsing behavior, cart abandonment, product preferences, and lifetime value. Use these insights to create targeted marketing campaigns and improve customer retention.
