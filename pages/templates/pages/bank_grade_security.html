We use a defense-in-depth approach with encryption, access controls, network segmentation, and AI-powered threat detection. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we maintain comprehensive audit logs.

What happens if there's a security incident?

Our 24/7 security operations center monitors for incidents and responds automatically. Critical incidents trigger immediate notifications to your security team, and we provide detailed incident reports and remediation guidance.

Yes, we provide APIs and integrations with popular security tools like SIEM systems, identity providers, and compliance platforms. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.

We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. Our privacy-by-design approach includes data minimization, purpose limitation, and automated compliance monitoring with regular privacy impact assessments.
