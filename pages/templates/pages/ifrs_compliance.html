IFRS Compliance FAQ

Answers to common questions about IFRS compliance and our automated solutions.

Which IFRS standards are supported?

We support all major IFRS standards including IFRS 9 (Financial Instruments), IFRS 15 (Revenue), IFRS 16 (Leases), IAS 36 (Impairment), and all other current standards with automatic updates as new standards are issued.

How do you handle IFRS implementation?

Our system automatically applies IFRS rules at the transaction level, ensuring compliance from the moment data is entered. Complex standards like IFRS 9 impairment calculations are handled through sophisticated algorithms.

Can the system generate XBRL filings?

Yes, Professional and Enterprise plans include automatic XBRL tagging for regulatory filings in multiple jurisdictions, ensuring your financial statements are ready for submission to stock exchanges and regulators.

What about local GAAP requirements?

Our platform supports dual reporting - you can maintain IFRS compliance while also generating local GAAP financial statements. The system handles the differences automatically.

How do you stay current with IFRS changes?

We maintain a dedicated IFRS standards team that monitors IASB updates and implements changes automatically. All clients receive updates without additional cost or disruption to their operations.
