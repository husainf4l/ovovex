Manufacturing FAQ

Answers to common questions from manufacturing companies.

How does job costing work for custom manufacturing?

Our job costing system tracks all direct and indirect costs for each production order, including materials, labor, overhead allocation, and variances. You get real-time visibility into profitability for every job.

Can I integrate with my existing MRP/ERP system?

Yes, we integrate with major MRP and ERP systems including SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, and Epicor. Our API-first architecture also supports custom integrations for specialized manufacturing software.

How do you handle different inventory valuation methods?

We support FIFO, LIFO, weighted average, and specific identification methods. You can use different valuation methods for different inventory categories and track cost of goods sold accordingly.

What kind of manufacturing analytics do you provide?

We provide comprehensive analytics including OEE tracking, production yield analysis, labor efficiency metrics, inventory optimization, and predictive cost modeling to help you optimize operations.

How do you handle work-in-progress (WIP) inventory?

Our system tracks WIP at every production stage with accurate cost accumulation. You can monitor progress, identify bottlenecks, and calculate completion percentages for better production planning.
