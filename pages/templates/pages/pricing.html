Pricing FAQ

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can explore all features during the trial period.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you approach your limits. You can upgrade your plan at any time, or pay for overages if you prefer to stay on your current plan.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. This discount is applied automatically at checkout when you select annual billing.

Can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
